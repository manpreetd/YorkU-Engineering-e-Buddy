DeltaCourses

Engineering offers numerous courses, all of which have a wide range of requirements that can be challenging to keep up with. You can rest easy now, as a York University student has made an informal course requirement map for all yearly courses. It has assisted me and numerous individuals greatly during course registration, streamlining the process of locating prerequisites and co-requisites for courses.



Course Checklist & Curriculum Updates

Lassonde every year may push changes to its engineering curriculums (course renames, new courses, etc). There may be changes I'm not aware of after I graduate. So it may not be feasible to keep updating this site, plus I don't want to spread misinformation. To see these changes go to the following page:

Pre-Enrolment Advising

Then scroll down to Section 2: Your Degree Requirements and click on Review Curriculum Updates and find your program.

Lassonde has also posted course checklists for each engineering program, found here when you scroll down to the academic years:

Program Checklists

You should also follow the checklist in the year when you entered the program in your first year. For instance, if you entered your first year in the 2024-25 academic year, you should follow the 2024-25 checklist (of course checking the curriculum updates every year too).



1st Year EECS Course Resources

EECS 1st Year Resources

The Lassonde Engineering Society has prepared a list of resources for 1st year EECS courses like EECS 1011, 1021 and 1028. If you want to be proficient or learning ahead for these courses, I recommend going through EECS 1021 and EECS 1028 as they are the most vital first year courses in Software Engineering.
